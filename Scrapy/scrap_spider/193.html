Media Release: The Biometrics Institute launches online job board

29 April 2016

Sydney/ London - The Biometrics Institute is proud to announce a new service available to its members and stakeholders of the biometrics industry with the launch of an online job board.

“We are excited that our members and industry stakeholders can now advertise biometrics related jobs via our job board,” states Isabelle Moeller, Chief Executive, Biometrics Institute, “it is a niche industry and we are representing the best global network of biometric experts. This is now an opportunity to tap into that network”. 

The job board strategically distributes posted jobs to 3rd party sites to attract more candidates and provides more exposure. Upgraded jobs will receive performance-based distribution to a network of over 1,000 job websites and search engines. 

The job board distributes each job by analysing data to show where the job posting is expected to attract the most candidates. In addition to the job posting being exposed to the most qualified candidates on the Biometrics Institute website, this new enhancement will give job postings optimal reach with up to 400% more responses.

As a peak industry association, the Biometrics Institute engages its large global membership through a range of networking opportunities including a range of events and now also through this job board.

Members and stakeholders can connect with other experts, from a cross-section of biometrics related sectors, for best-practice and innovation exchange.  Please refer to the job board website for more information. Further details of upcoming Institute events can be found here.

The Biometrics Institute is the independent and impartial international forum for biometrics users and other interested parties.  It has been established to promote the responsible use of biometrics technologies. The Biometrics Institute has offices in Australia and the UK and a member base of over 200 organisations worldwide, represented by close to 800 individuals from 26 countries.
