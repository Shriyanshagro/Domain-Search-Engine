Storyline

In her opening monologue, host Chelsea Handler talks about what she did today at the studio on this the last day of the show, including the last communal shower she had in the Chelsea Lately shower facility. Chelsea and Chuy bring on her current stable of round table regulars. What Chelsea believes is going to be three of her closest celebrity friends, Mary McCormack - her best friend - Jennifer Aniston, and Sandra Bullock, providing a gracious send-off ends up being what they hope will be more useful for Chelsea's life. Chelsea's ex-lover 50 Cent and G-Unit perform their new single, "Watch Me". Chuy, via a prepared video, talks about what this show and Chelsea have meant to him. Miley Cyrus, in tribute to it being the last show, performs the pop classic, "It's Over". And many of Chelsea's musical friends, and some non-musical friends, help her serenade E!, to whom she is grateful for the past seven years. Written by Huggo
