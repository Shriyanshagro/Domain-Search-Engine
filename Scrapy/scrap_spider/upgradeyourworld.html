        We want to help you do great things

        #UpgradeYourWorld

        Microsoft supports organization that #UpgradeYourWorldUSA

        Win a $50,000 donation, technology and more.

        If you represent a charity active only in your country, you could be selected under our #UpgradeYourWorldUSA initiative. From 1 September – 23 September, we’re asking people around the world to vote for their favourite local nonprofit organization. The organization with the most votes will receive a $50,000 USD donation plus technology and support from Microsoft. Make sure that your organization qualifies by reviewing the official rules here.

        Yournonprofit@insertyournonprofit

        Help us do great things and win $50,000 USD. Vote for us by tagging us @insertyournonprofit and using hashtags #UpgradeYourWorldUSA #Vote. #UpgradeYourWorldUSA #Vote.

        Tweet this

        How can we participate?

        It’s easy. The organization with the most votes wins, so encourage your supporters to vote for you. Make sure they know they can vote once per day on participating social channels* and this website. Ensure they are tagging you in their posts and including the hash tags #UpgradeYourWorldUSA and #Vote to make their votes count. *Twitter and Instagram

        Promotion

        You may wish to post information about #UpgradeYourWorldUSA on your social channels or include in a newsletter or on a website. We’ve included some copy examples below to help you get started with spreading the word and rallying your supporters.

        Sample social copy:

        Help us do great things and win $50,000 USD. Vote for us by tagging us @insertyournonprofit and using hashtags #UpgradeYourWorldUSA #Vote.

        Sample newsletter copy:

        Help us #UpgradeYourWorldUSA and do more to <insert organization mission>. <Insert name of org> has the chance to win $50,000 USD and technology and support from Microsoft. Public voting will determine the winner so please vote for us each day using #UpgradeYourWorldUSA and #Vote. For more information, visit Windows.com/UpgradeYourWorld.
