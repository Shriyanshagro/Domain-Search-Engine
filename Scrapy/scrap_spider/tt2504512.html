Storyline

To Joris's horror, his scorned would be-bride Joyce has induced an abortion and now blames him. Pushed by his mates to date again, but the girl refuses to start a relationship because he's 'too gorgeous'. Boduewijn is terrified to discover his car inadvertently hit 'rival' Emiel, who landed in hospital, and Sophie enlists him to search for the culprit. When Tamar meanly dumps David's souvenir belongings, he cuts her off financially. Her lawyer however finds a loophole in their prenuptial to claim half of his firm. Written by KGF Vissers
