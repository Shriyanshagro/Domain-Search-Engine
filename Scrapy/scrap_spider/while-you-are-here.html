Police registration

Nationals of certain countries who come to live in the UK for more than six months need to register with the Police.

If you need to register with the Police it will normally say so on your visa or Biometric Residence Permit.

You can find out if your country is on the Police Registration list by checking Appendix 2 of the Immigration Rules.

Appendix 2 of the Immigration Rules 

How to register

If you do not register with the Police on-campus, you will need to go to the Police Headquarters in Edinburgh.

Police Headquarters

Nationality Department, Police Headquarters, Fettes Avenue, Edinburgh, EH4 1RB

Opening times are Monday-Friday 9am-12.30pm

You will need:

  • Your passport
  • One passport-sized photograph
  • Your visa or Biometric Residence Permit
  • Evidence of your address in Edinburgh
  • £34 cash payment

On-campus registration

If you arrive to the University in September, you may be able to register with the Police on-campus.

We invite the Police’s Nationality Unit to the University each September.  Details will be made available closer to the time, and you will be able to book an appointment online.

Updating your Police Registration

If Police Registration is a condition of your visa, you must update your details with the Police each time you change your address or get new immigration permission.

You can update your Police Registration Certificate at any Police Station.
