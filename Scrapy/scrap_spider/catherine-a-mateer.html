Catherine A. Mateer

Catherine A. Mateer was appointed to a second five-year term as the associate vice-president academic planning 2011. Prior to this, she served as the director of clinical training for the Graduate Program in Clinical Psychology and as the chair of the Department of Psychology.  She is well known internationally for her pioneering work in cognitive rehabilitation.   As AVP Academic Planning, Dr. Mateer is responsible for the quality assurance of academic programs, the approval of new academic programs, oversight of the Learning and Teaching Centre and Co-operative Education and Career Services.  She has also chaired UVic’s Educational Technology Advisory Council and led a review of institutional support for technology integrated learning.
