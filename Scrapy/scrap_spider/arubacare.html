Find out all the ways we care with ArubaCare.

Get the dedicated support you need, when you need it.

Count on ArubaCare Next-Day Support.

Get the ultimate in customer care around the clock and the world with ArubaCare Next-Day Support.

Engineered to support.

Get unlimited access to our rigorously trained technical support engineers with ArubaTAC access. We’ll respond to email or phone calls within one hour, round the clock, 365 days a year.

Stay on top of software maintenance.

ArubaCare Support customers have exclusive access to all software releases, major and minor. All software is available for download through the Aruba Support Center.

Aruba Support Center is here to help.

Get 24×7 access to critical tech info, like FAQs, field alerts, release notes, product documentation and updates and upgrades.

Replace hardware by tomorrow.

Get any replacement part by the next business day. Just return the defective one to us as soon as you get it.

Count on Premium Support.

Get the highest level of priority support from our elite certified mobility engineers. Choose from four Premium Support packages to meet your needs.

Bring on a Resident Engineer.

Bring highly customized onsite expertise to your own network IT staff. Your Resident Engineer is dedicated to helping you avoid common network pitfalls, proactively fix problems, and access resources at Aruba.
