Business Wire NewsHQsm: your online newsroom. Conversations start here.

Manage Your Communications Center

Business Wire NewsHQsm is a secure Web-based Content Management System (CMS) that empowers you to create and update a branded online newsroom.

With NewsHQ, you can post breaking news, press releases, multimedia, executive biographies, press kits, events, brand articles and stories, and much more, all within one secure, central news headquarters.

Advanced management tools, high-capacity servers, search engine optimization, social media engagement features and measurement analytics provide valuable resources for you and all of your audiences - media, bloggers, consumers and key influencers.
