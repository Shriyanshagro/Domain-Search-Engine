Podcast: She Said in conversation – “dance cuts across culture and boundaries”

Posted on 15 Mar 2016, 13:58 by English National Ballet

Categories: Featured, News, Productions

Tags: She Said

Opening next month at Sadler’s Wells, our ground-breaking new triple bill She Said features three new ballets by Aszure Barton, Annabelle Lopez Ochoa and Yabin Wang.

Ahead of the world première, we brought together a panel of influential women to discuss how women can act to challenge the status quo and break down barriers in the arts and business worlds.

Listen back to Artistic Director Tamara Rojo in discussion alongside Executive Director Caroline Thomson, Partner in international law firm Dechert Miriam Durántez González, Chief Operating Officer at Bank of England Charlotte Hogg and journalist and presenter of BBC Radio 4 World at One Martha Kearney.

She Said In Conversation took place on Tuesday 9 February 2016 at the London offices of Aviva.

  • She Said runs at Sadler’s Wells, London 13 – 16 April 2016.
  • Read more behind-the-scenes blogs and articles about She Said
  • Follow the creation of She Said with our special Storify of tweets and images from our dancers and choreographers.
