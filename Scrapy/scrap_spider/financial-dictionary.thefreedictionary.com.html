UYN

ISO 4217 code for the Uruguayan nuevo peso. It replaced the first peso at a rate of 1,000 old pesos to one nuevo peso. It was subject to high inflation toward the end of its circulation. It was replaced by the peso uruguayo at a rate of 1,000 nuevo pesos to one peso uruguayo.
