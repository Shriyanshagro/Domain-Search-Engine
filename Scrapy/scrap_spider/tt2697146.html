Storyline

In her opening monologue, host Chelsea Handler sends a message to all her staff that have offspring. Chelsea and her round table panelists Dov Davidoff, Annie Lederman and Ross Mathews discuss Kelly Clarkson's beef with Clive Davis over his depiction of their collaboration in his memoirs, a $50 million diamond heist at Brussels Airport, a man falling out his eighth story window while trying to visit friends as his wife locked him in their bedroom, and a South Dakota postal delivery man being caught drunk on the job. In their next Oscar nominated Best Picture movie review, the Keshishians talk about Argo (2012). And Chelsea chats with guest Justin Bartha about his show The New Normal (2012), working with people that Chelsea considers total messes, most specifically Christopher Walken, his friend Bradley Cooper's Oscar nomination, and completing filming of The Hangover Part III (2013). Written by Huggo
