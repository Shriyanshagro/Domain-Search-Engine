Joyce Unleashed

  • Vanessa Anspaugh. Photo: Steve Niedorf
  • Elina Pirinen. Photo: Timo Wright
  • Anna Sperber. Photo: Ian Douglas
Description

The Joyce moves beyond its Chelsea walls with these engagements designed to give audiences the chance to see cutting-edge artists performing in intimate spaces. This season we present Elina Pirinen and Anna Sperber at New York Live Arts and Vanessa Anspaugh at Abrons Arts Center.

Elina Pirinen May 31-Jun 1, 2016
Anna Sperber Jun 3-4, 2016
Vanessa Anspaugh Jun 8-11, 2016

Funding Credits
Major support for The Joyce’s presentation of emerging and experimental artists through Joyce Unleashed provided by The Harkness Foundation for Dance. Support for The Joyce’s presentation of Elina Pirinen provided by the Consulate General of Finland in New York.
Performances are subject to change.
