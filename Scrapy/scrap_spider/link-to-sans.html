About: Link to SANS

Like SANS? Spread the word and show your support for our online security community! Linking to SANS is easy and lets others know where to go for the #1 stop in computer security training, information, and research.

Pick a badge and copy the single line of text right below it. Simply paste this code wherever you would like it to show up. You can drop a badge on your website, blog or Twitter page. Add it as a customized link on your Facebook profile. If you would like to host the image yourself, no problem. Just right-click on the graphic and then click save.
