Classroom

Get industry-leading instruction in a professional classroom experience.

Enjoy the benefits of Red Hat classroom training:

  • Instructors are experienced professionals who are very knowledgeable about the topics they teach
  • Courses actively engage students in task-focused activities, lab-based knowledge checks, and facilitative discussions
  • Course materials provide job aids, exercise reinforcement, and task-based reference material
  • Classrooms are equipped with dedicated system(s) for each student
  • Courses are also available onsite for private team training

Virtual Training

Instructor-led, real-time training in an online environment.

Red Hat virtual classroom training is real-time training conducted by live instructors in an online environment. You get the same industry-respected content and hands-on labs as the corresponding classroom-based courses.

Red Hat Online Training

Your pace, any place. Log in and learn.

With Red Hat Online Learning, you get:

  • A 90-day subscription. Provides unlimited user access to Red Hat Online Learning course content and webinars
  • 80-hours of online, hands-on lab access, deployed through Amazon EC2 cloud
  • Preparation a Red Hat certification exam.
  • Online course materials created and supported by Red Hat curriculum architects.

Red Hat Certifications

Real skills, real systems. Not just quiz questions.

Since 1999, our certification program has helped IT professionals prove their skills and build their IT career. It also gives employers a meaningful way to find and develop qualified professionals.

All that is required to earn a Red Hat certification is a hands-on, practical exam using Red Hat technologies in a real-world scenario.

Have questions about the certification program? Are you ready to book an exam? Contact us today.

The Value of Red Hat Certification

Employers Value

  • An efficient way to identify people with the skills they need
  • A way to mitigate risks associated with the new projects and technologies
  • A way to develop and retain a high-performing team by benchmarking skills

IT Professionals Value

  • A way to differentiate themselves
  • A way to verify their own skills and knowledge
  • A way to demonstrate readiness for new responsibilities

All Red Hat certifications and Certificates of Expertise are earned by passing one or more hands-on, practical exams using Red Hat technologies

Red Hat Certified professionals are proven

Red Hat certifications are earned by passing rigorous, hands-on exams.

Certifications help managers identify, develop, and retain professionals who are skilled, proven, and ready to work on Red Hat technologies.
