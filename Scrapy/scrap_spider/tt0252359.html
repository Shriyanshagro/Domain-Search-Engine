Daughter of Suicide (2000)

Plot Summary

    Showing one plot summary

  • Daughter of Suicide is a groundbreaking personal documentary which explores the topics of suicide and depression in an honest and eye-opening manner. It reveals the reality of one mother's life cut short by a disease called depression and the effect of this suicide on her daughter, her family and her friends.

    - Written by Dempsey Rice
