About the CIKC

CIKC is a centre of excellence for low temperature processing using  materials, such as polymers, advanced liquid crystals, metal oxides and nanostructures for applications in computers, displays and communications.

CIKC’s mission is to accelerate the commercial exploitation of early stage university technology in partnership with industry – bridging the gap between academic research and commercial exploitation.

CIKC is part of the EPSRC Centre for Innovative Manufacturing in Large-Area Electronics and one of the partners in the European COLAE project.

Our technology

CIKC has expertise in low temperature processing of organic and inorganic materials including:

  • flexible photovoltaics
  • flexible displays and lighting
  • printed electronics and components
  • smart objects
  • polymer waveguide systems
  • liquid crystal on silicon
  • transparent electronics and sensors
