Young Artists
Training
Programme

Young Artists Training Programme launched with the opening of the Opera Studio in 1998. With the addition of the Ballet School in 2001 and the Drama Studio in 2005,
fulltime training is conducted to foster opera singers, ballet dancers and actors of tomorrow. Each division provides a comprehensive curriculum in specialised techniques, extensive knowledge and cultural refinement by faculty of the highest standard. The Training Programme run by the New National Theatre, Tokyo, is designed to foster the next generation of talented young artists, complete with opportunities for students to demonstrate their skills on stage.
