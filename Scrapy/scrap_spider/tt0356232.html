Storyline

"BingoLotto" is the most famous game show in Sweden. There's several ways to win prizes or money, the most obvious one is that you get bingo. When you get bingo you automatically win a standard sum of money, but you also get the chance to call to the show to win other things. One of the most wanted prizes is a brand new Volvo.
