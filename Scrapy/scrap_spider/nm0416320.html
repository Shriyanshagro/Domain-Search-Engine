Brian James (I) (1918–2009)

Actor
Brian James was born on July 5, 1918 in Maryborough, Victoria, Australia. He was an actor, known for Skyways (1979), A Cry in the Dark (1988) and Emergency (1959). He died on November 2, 2009 in Prahran, Melbourne, Victoria. See full bio »
