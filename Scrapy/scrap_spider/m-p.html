6/6 - Boost Software ROI with ART and the new ART Enterprise Learning

[ Edited ]

﻿

Deep Dive Session V9608, offered by Vivit Worldwide and hosted by HPE will be at Discover 2016 in LV on June 6.  Boost Software ROI with ART and the new ART Enterprise Learning - will show capabilities and best practices of the Adoption Readiness Tool (ART) for a new style of learning that raises user adoption and more.  Register for this session through your new or existing Discover 2016 LV conference registration for $99.

More information on vivit Deep Dives:  http://bit.ly/1S77BHH
