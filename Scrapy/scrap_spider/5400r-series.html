5400R Series

Performance and power for the mobile-first campus

  • 5400R Switch Series Data Sheet
  • Take advantage of 802.11ac Wave 2 with HPE Smart Rate multi-gigabit Ethernet
Contact Sales
  • Benefits
  • Specifications
    Benefits

  • Powerful Layer 3 modular switch series that delivers high-performance, low latency and resiliency for a better mobile-first campus experience. With industry standard OpenFlow, the 5400R is ready for SDN applications such as HPE Network Visualizer, Optimizer, and Protector.

    Using a powerful ProVision ASIC to deliver wire-speed classification and policy enforcement, the 5400R brings enterprise-class resiliency and innovative flexibility and scalability for a better mobile-first campus network experience.

    • 802.11ac-ready wired network

      Multi-gigabit Ethernet with PoE+ for high speed 802.11ac APs using existing cabling.

    • Resiliency and high availability

      Nonstop switching and routing with low latency and wire speed 40GbE for wireless traffic aggregation.

    • Single view of the network

      Consistent wired and wireless experience with ClearPass Policy Manager and AirWave Network Management.
