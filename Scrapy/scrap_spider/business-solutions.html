    The latest range of Lumia phones

    The latest Microsoft Lumia phones are packed with the latest features, to get the most out of Windows 10. The Microsoft Lumia 950 and 950 XL support Windows Hello Beta, an exciting innovation that uses biometric information to unlock your phone. These devices also support mobile docking and wireless charging, and have the latest features you’d expect from a business phone.1 And with a commitment from Microsoft to continuous updates and innovations, you’ll always have the best mobile experience.2

    Lumia 950
    Lumia 950 XL

    Enterprise-grade management and security

    Mobile device management (MDM) Windows 10 for phone supports Microsoft Intune and other compatible third-party MDM solutions, so you can distribute apps and updates to your Microsoft Lumia devices automatically.

    Learn more about security on Lumia

    Getting down to business

    Involved in running a small or medium-sized business? Let Lumia go to work for you and help your employees get tasks done. Then watch the productivity flow. Lumia smartphones come with an entire range of key business features for your company’s needs, while business apps and Microsoft services allow you and your staff to not only get more done faster, but also get there faster with maps and navigation built in.

    Productivity comes built in

    Lumia means working smart, not working more. Ready to support your business, Lumia powers on to the world of Microsoft services. Office 365 with Word, Excel, PowerPoint and more is there when and where you need it. Access and edit important documents on the go with OneDrive for Business and get all the core Outlook functionality for mobile – email, calendar, notes and contacts are automatically synced across all your employees’ devices.

    Learn about productivity on Lumia

    Data protection for less worries

    World-class mobile device security isn’t optional. It’s imperative. All apps in our Store are screened for malware before they’re published, Internet Explorer warns about known malicious websites, and employees’ work documents are safely stored to the cloud for easy access on OneDrive for Business.

    Read more about Lumia security
