Brad Bird (I)

Miscellaneous Crew | Writer | Director
Brad Bird was born on September 24, 1957 in Kalispell, Montana, USA as Phillip Bradley Bird. He is known for his work on Ratatouille (2007), The Incredibles (2004) and The Simpsons (1989). He has been married to Elizabeth Canney since 1988. They have three children. See full bio »
