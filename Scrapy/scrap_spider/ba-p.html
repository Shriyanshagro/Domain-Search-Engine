Myth vs reality: Advanced analytics for security operations

StephanieWisdom on ‎04-29-2016 07:36 AM

The ever-evolving threat landscape is top-of-mind for everyone these days. Our recent State of Security Operations report, showed us that there is still much to be done in the way of enterprise security, risk management, and security intelligence. As HPE Discover 2016 in Las Vegas approaches, HPE Security is committed to offering you sessions that can futher expand your knowledge and help you protect your digital enterprise.

﻿

As a security solutions provider, we know, first-hand, how IT and security operations teams face the growing challenge of sifting through large volumes of security information and event data gathered from their infrastructure. We also understand that pervasive and adaptable security requires...no, DEMANDS, proactive detection and fast responses to all threats.

With that knowledge at hand, we invite you to attend breakout session BB9221, "Advanced analytics for security operations: Myth vs reality." In this session, you will examine best practices for applying rigorous analytics to an intelligent operations center. You will explore a wide range of analytics, from the real-time correlation of threat intelligence to investigation to proactive hunting of threat actors and their activities. 

It's time for a reality check. Join us at HPE Discover 2016, and learn how to protect your digital enterprise. 

Follow @HPE_Security and LinkedIn.

Follow @HPE_Discover and the hashtag #HPEDiscover to stay up-to-date on all HPE Discover activities.
