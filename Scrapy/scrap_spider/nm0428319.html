Jason Jones (I)

Actor | Writer | Producer
Jason Jones was born on June 3, 1973 in Hamilton, Ontario, Canada. He is an actor and writer, known for The Daily Show (1996), Pitch Perfect 2 (2015) and Pitch Perfect (2012). He has been married to Samantha Bee since October 13, 2001. They have three children. See full bio »
