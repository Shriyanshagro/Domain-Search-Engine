ITT Corporation

ITT is a leading manufacturer of highly engineered, customized solutions for the energy, transportation and industrial markets. Our legacy of innovation is reflected in our trusted products and brands including Goulds and Bornemann pumps, KONI shock absorbers, Cannon connectors and Enidine energy absorption devices. We continue to build on this heritage and to partner with customers to solve their most critical problems.

Our Businesses

ITT is composed of four businesses – Industrial Process, Motion Technologies, Interconnect Solutions and Control Technologies – that are working in close harmony and leveraging their collective power to better serve the energy, transportation and industrial end markets.

OUR PRODUCTS

We partner closely with our customers to solve their most critical problems with highly engineered and customized solutions. Our portfolio of products is balanced and diverse and aligned with enduring global growth drivers such as urbanization and sustainable development.

Our Brands

ITT has long-standing brands that are respected and trusted globally. These brands – from our advanced Goulds Pumps and KONI shock absorbers, to our harsh-environment Cannon connectors and Enidine energy absorption devices – represent our legacy of technological innovation.
