Hannelore Elsner

Actress | Soundtrack | Costume Designer
Hannelore Elsner was born on July 26, 1942 in Burghausen, Germany as Hannelore Elstner. She is an actress, known for Lady Cop (1994), Cherry Blossoms (2008) and No Place to Go (2000). She was previously married to Uwe Carstensen and Gerd Vespermann. See full bio »
