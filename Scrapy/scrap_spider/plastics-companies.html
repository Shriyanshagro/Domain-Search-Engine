Machine Tools & Injection Moulding Machines


MJH machine tools have been a firmly established CNC machine tool supplier for the better half of two decades. Here at MJH we offer a wide range of CNC machining centers, lathes, surface grinders, wire cut EDMs, Injection moulders and many more.

Our suppliers, except for our Haixing agents, are all situated in Taiwan which is fast becoming the leader in export of quality machine tools.

Our client base is stretched across South Africa, with offices in Durban and Johannesburg and agents Port Elizabeth and Cape Town.
