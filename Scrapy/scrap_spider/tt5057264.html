Storyline

This ostensible documentary short subject features "mailman" Eddie Borden delivering mail to various Hollywood stars including Richard Arlen and his wife Jobyna Ralston, Tom Mix, Bing Crosby, and Mary Pickford. Eddie really wants to be offered something to drink, but no one seems to pick up on his desire. Pickford gives him a lift to the studio, where Eddie is left high and dry. Written by Jim Beaver <jumblejim@prodigy.net>

Hollywood on Parade No. A-4 (1933)

*** (out of 4)

Eddie Borden plays a postal worker who decides to deliver some mail to some Hollywood legends and he ends up at the studio where he visits more legends. Richard Arlen, Bing Crosby, Mary Pickford and Tom Mix are some of the famous faces that are on display in this fun entry in the series. The highlight is certainly the segment with Pickford as she talks about a package she received from China. There's also a nice scene between her and Crosby. Obviously there's not much of a "story" here but the main point of this was to show off the stars and seeing them together today is certainly a nice thing to have.
