Storyline

Meego is a 9,000 year old alien whose spaceship crashes on Earth and is discovered by Trip, Maggie, and Alex Parker. They take a liking to him and convince their single father, Edward, to take him in as a nanny. He agrees to stay until his ship is fixed, but eventually grows attached to the children and remains on Earth. Written by Ondre Lombard <olombard@lombard.cyberverse.com>

Good-hearted but half-baked
28 August 2002 | by Nozz (Israel) – See all my reviews

Nothing wrong with a TV series built on moral fables. In fact, _Perfect Strangers_ was full of moral fables beneath its vaudeville humor. But _Meego_ wasn't fully conceived. There wasn't enough of a sense of limit to Meego's magical powers, and in general there wasn't enough underlying tension or conflict to drive the series from episode to episode.
