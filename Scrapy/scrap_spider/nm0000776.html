Michael Apted

Director | Producer | Miscellaneous Crew
Michael Apted was born on February 10, 1941 in Aylesbury, Buckinghamshire, England as Michael David Apted. He is a director and producer, known for The World Is Not Enough (1999), The Chronicles of Narnia: The Voyage of the Dawn Treader (2010) and Enough (2002). See full bio »
