Performing

While the primary focus of Winter Term training remains the classroom, SAB is involved in several activities that provide students with the opportunity to learn and perform notable choreography.

Many students enrolled in the Children's Division receive opportunities to perform with New York City Ballet, while Advanced Students participate in SAB's annual Student Workshop. Selected Intermediate and Advanced students gain additional performing experience through NYCB's Lecture-Demonstration program.

The experience of rehearsing and performing for audiences at any age enables students to develop confidence and stage manners, which will be essential to their future professional careers.
