Work effectively and more securely anywhere, on any device

In today's interconnected world, you need the freedom to work effectively and securely, wherever you are. Empower your team to meet online and work together from anywhere on shared projects. Remotely access files and customer information. Easily manage a range of devices. Microsoft’s trusted productivity solutions, with enterprise-grade protection built-in, provide a familiar mobile experience across your business applications and devices.

Microsoft offers a familiar platform that combines the best of mobile productivity with the protection and control your business needs. Cloud-based Office 365 empowers your team to meet and collaborate online, and revise documents across locations, using trusted productivity tools. Combined with innovative Window 10 Pro devices, it is easy for small and midsized businesses to protect business data, enable secure remote access and collaboration, and manage mobile devices.

Office 365 offers an integrated and cloud-based suite of productivity tools. With Outlook and Skype for Business, you can easily connect with customers and collaborate with colleagues. Using familiar applications, such as Word, Excel, and PowerPoint, you can create, edit and share documents from any device. Sync those files across all your devices with OneDrive for Business.

Safeguard your business

Enterprise-grade protection anywhere you do business

Missing mobile devices can result in lost company files. Malicious hackers or malware can disrupt business and steal critical data. Systems can go down unexpectedly. Microsoft technologies have protections - built-in - to help safeguard data across devices and apps, onsite or in the cloud; help you recover from business disruptions; and make compliance easier. You don't have to buy anything extra. With Microsoft, security is just there.

Get enterprise-grade protection for your small or midsized business. A cloud-based solution, including Microsoft Azure, gives you simple, reliable backup and disaster recovery. Combined with Windows 10 Pro, you can better protect business information, identities, data and network connections -- from desktop and devices to cloud. Because these safeguards are built-in, management is easy and you're protected anywhere you do business.

Microsoft Azure is a flexible cloud platform that helps you grow your business with greater efficiency and be more responsive to change. Your business critical data is easily backed up to the cloud and restored with Azure Backup - providing scalable, secure backup services with up to 99 years of data retention for legal compliance.

Windows 10 Pro provides enterprise-grade security for small and midsized business, with built-in protection for identities, data, and devices. Two-factor password alternatives better protect identities. Enterprise-class data protection helps secure data wherever it is. And hardware-based security helps safeguard against malware.

Grow efficiently with Microsoft products

Reduce costs as you grow your business

You've got big plans and you need technology that can grow right along with your business. Technology that adjusts quickly to changing business needs, maximizes your existing IT investment, and does not require additional management. With Microsoft’s flexible, cloud-based technology platform you can have the business tools of a much larger company, at the right price.

Reduce your IT budget with technology that adapts to your small and midsized business needs. Using a cloud-based technology solution including Microsoft Azure and Office 365, you can be more responsive to change and simplify routine IT tasks from cloud to devices. Easily add users, storage, and apps on demand. Pay only for the technology and services you use. Move to the cloud, while getting the most out of your existing investments. With Microsoft, you get the right technology for every phase of your business.

Microsoft Azure is a flexible cloud platform that helps you grow your business with greater efficiency and be more responsive to change. With Microsoft Azure, you can be up and running fast, scale up or down as needed, and avoid high capital costs—paying only for what you use. And you can make the cloud work for your business, with the options to quickly move applications from on-site servers to the cloud, build new “cloud-native” applications, or take a hybrid approach.
