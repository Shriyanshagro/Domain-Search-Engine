    The Official Kauffman Stadium App

    Unlock offers and get exclusive in-game features

    MLB.com At the Ballpark ®



    MLB.com Ballpark for iPhone and Android

    The MLB.com Ballpark app is your mobile companion when visiting Kauffman Stadium. The official MLB ballpark application perfectly complements and personalizes your trip to Kauffman Stadium with mobile check-in, social media, offers, rewards and exclusive content. Kauffman Stadium also offers seat and experience upgrade components. Check in automatically with iBeacon technology (iOS devices only) at Kauffman Stadium for exclusive Royals offers and in-game features.

    Features:

    • Unlock offers and check in to games using iBeacon technology
    • Access your online tickets with My Tickets
    • Upgrade your seat and ballpark experience
    • View your check-in history and access exclusive content with the Journal
    • Explore the park with interactive concourse maps, directories and concession menus
    • Find out your favorite player and ballpark entrance music, with the ability to purchase songs on iPhone
    • Access club rewards programs

     

    * iOS requires iOS 7.0 and later. Android requires 4.0 and later
