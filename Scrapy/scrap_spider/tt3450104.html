Storyline

In her opening monologue, host Chelsea Handler talks about the new freedom she is feeling and how she would like it to manifest itself. Chelsea and her round table panelists Kerri Kenney-Silver, Bobby Lee and Josh Wolf discuss Toronto mayor Rob Ford falling off the alcohol sobriety bandwagon, another polar vortex plunging temperatures down on the North American east coast, and Trace Adkins not performing his headlining set on a country cruise instead checking himself into rehab which pissed off his fans on-board the cruise they who felt they were not adequately compensated by the cruise line who offered one hour worth of free drinks. And Chelsea chats with guest Gabourey Sidibe about her appearance on the latest season of American Horror Story (2011), her rebuttal to criticisms about the way she looked at 71st Golden Globe Awards (2014), living in New Orleans during filming of the show, going on a swamp tour, and getting into a bar fight. Written by Huggo
