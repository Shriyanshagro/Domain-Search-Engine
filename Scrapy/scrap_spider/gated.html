Enterprise Strategy Group - Lab Validation of Fortinet ATP Framework

Security breaches are becoming increasingly common—with subsequent costs that include not just resuming operations and addressing security gaps, but legal liability and regulatory fines.

The Enterprise Strategy Group (ESG) Lab Validation Report documents hands-on testing of Fortinet Advanced Threat Protection (ATP) Framework—an end-to-end modular solution for strengthening cybersecurity to tackle the newest threats. In this report, ESG’s testing explores how Fortinet satisfies today’s critical security requirements, such as:

  • Providing intelligent, actionable threat information to improve the security posture of enterprises large and small
  • Detecting unknown threats, accelerating response processes, and preventing known attacks
  • Ensuring coverage of multiple threat vectors and functions within a comprehensive solution that’s highly automated and easy to understand and manage
