Richard Donner

Director | Producer | Actor
Richard Donner was born on April 24, 1930 in New York City, New York, USA as Richard D. Schwartzberg. He is a director and producer, known for Lethal Weapon (1987), The Goonies (1985) and Lethal Weapon 2 (1989). He has been married to Lauren Shuler Donner since November 27, 1986. See full bio »
