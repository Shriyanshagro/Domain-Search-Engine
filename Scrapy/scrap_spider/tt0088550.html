Larry King Live (1985– )

Plot Summary

    Showing all 2 plot summaries

  • Long-running news/talk show hosted by TV veteran Larry King. Each week, King brings on a series of guests for in depths, one-on-one interviews. His choice of guests is often controversial, sometimes unpopular, but almost always newsworthy.

    - Written by Jean-Marc Rocher <rocher@fiberbit.net>

  • Popular and controversial journalist Larry King hosts this CNN call-in talk show.

    - Written by Tad Dibbern <DIBBERN_D@a1.mscf.upenn.edu>
