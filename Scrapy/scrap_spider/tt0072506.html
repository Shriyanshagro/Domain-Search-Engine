Storyline

ABC's "Good Morning America" presents the News and Information Source of the day's topics and journalism. The 1st Co-Host Team of David Hartman & Nancy Dussault, 2nd was Sandy Hill, 3rd was Joan Lunden, 4th Charles "Charlie" Gibson, 5th Lisa McKree, 6th Kevin Newman, 7th Diane Sawyer, and now Robin Roberts, George Stephanopoulos, and Lara Spencer. Plus, all the "Good Morning America" team throughout the years to wake up to the news show and entertainment about the U.S.A. Written by Ben Hallums<ben21house@yahoo.com>

The BEST morning show ever!
10 December 2001 | by drok2006 (Pennsylvania) – See all my reviews

This show is the best for all of the news, and what is new and hip in today's world. Tony Perkins is always somewhere different doing the weather, and I am glad that the show takes him places and helps him not only be a weatherman, but a reporter as well.

Charles Gibson and Diane Sawyer are very good as the host of this show, and their jokes, while sometimes off-key, are still funny. Sometimes, you can even hear the crew laughing at them during the broadcast.

Emeril has been a great addition as well, and he is always doing something in the 1st floor studio, whether cooking or having a contest, is always interesting. You always see the reporters doing things hands-on, which never is really seen with the other shows. So, in general, if you are watching some other morning show, you should be watching Good Morning, America instead.
