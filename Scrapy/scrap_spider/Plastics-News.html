MJH Machine Tools Johannesburg open show day 18 - 21 August

Published: : 2015-06-25

MJH Machine Tools will be holding their Johannesburg open show day from 18 – 21 August 2015.
We will have our servo injection moulding machine and robot on display for viewing. We will also be showing our full range of cnc machines from lathes to machining centres at the same time.

For more information kindly email janet@mjhsa.co.za for an official invite.

Alternatively contact our JHB office on 010 005 0634
