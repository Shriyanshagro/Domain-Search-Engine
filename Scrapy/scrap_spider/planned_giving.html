Planned Giving

The School of American Ballet is committed to training talented young dancers for professional ballet careers. Planned Gifts to the School of American Ballet help ensure that SAB continues to produce the next generations of dancers well into the future. Friends who make planned gifts to SAB are invited to join the Founders Society, a special group of farsighted supporters.

For more information about how Planned Gifts can benefit you, your family, and the School of American Ballet, please explore these pages or contact the Individual Giving office at 212-769-6614 or development@sab.org. All inquires are confidential.

This information about planned giving is provided solely for educational purposes. Please consult your own legal and financial advisors before making a major gift.
