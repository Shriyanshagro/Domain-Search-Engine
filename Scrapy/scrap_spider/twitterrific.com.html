Twitterrific for iOS

  • Free Download! For iPhone and iPad

Twitterrific is also available for Mac.

  • Fast & Light

    A beautifully designed, all-new user interface that doesn't get in the way makes tweeting quick & easy.

  • Timeline Sync

    Save your timeline reading position between iPhone and iPad with Tweet Marker or iCloud.

  • Push Notifications

    Never miss a Direct Message or Mention again. Twitterrific notifies you of the tweets that matter most.

  • Theme Control

    Choose light or dark themes, effortlessly change fonts, type & avatar sizes, adjust line spacing and more.

  • Muffle Tweets

    Muffle usernames, hashtags, and domains to keep your timeline free from spoilers and unwanted tweets.

  • Unified Timeline

    Color-coding lets you quickly scan your timeline for tweets that are important to you like mentions and direct messages.

  • People Search

    Find your friends and favorite celebrities in just a few taps with Twitterrific's powerful people search.

  • VoiceOver

    Robust support for Apple's VoiceOver means Twitterrific is easily accessible to the visually impaired.

  • Save for Later

    Quickly send any link to Instapaper, Pocket, Pinboard, or Safari Reading List for convenient reading later on.

Twitterrific at a glance.

Twitterrific has landed on Apple Watch!

  • Track your Twitter stats in real time, including RTs, favs, and new followers, via Twitterrific's glance view.
  • View a list of your most recent activity. Reply to mentions, direct messages, and more via dictation.
  • Receive notifications, follow new users, and favorite tweets, all from your wrist.

Full Apple Watch functionality requires Twitterrific's in-app push notifications.
