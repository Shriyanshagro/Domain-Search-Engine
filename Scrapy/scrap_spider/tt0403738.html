Storyline

Even the world's best-known celebrities can have bad days and occasionally they are caught misbehaving on tape. For the first time, look at the footage of celebrities at their worst behaviour, as they abuse the paparazzi, make fools of themselves with drunken and disorderly behaviour, and step out without the guidance of their highly-paid fashion stylist.
