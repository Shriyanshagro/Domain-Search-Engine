Bill Moyers

Writer | Editorial Department | Producer
Bill Moyers was born on June 5, 1934 in Hugo, Oklahoma, USA. He is known for his work on Bill Moyers' Journal (1972), Creativity with Bill Moyers (1982) and Bill Moyers on Faith & Reason (2006). He has been married to Judith Davidson Moyers since December 18, 1954. They have three children. See full bio »
