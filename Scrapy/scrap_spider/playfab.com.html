The PlayFab Promise

Building a successful “game-as-a-service” is hard. We know—we’ve been there. It’s why we founded this company. We want you to focus on the fun, not worry about success.

We’re Safe

Your players and your data are yours. If you ever leave, take them with you. In the meantime, they’re protected by multiple levels of backups and redundancies.

View data download API »

We’re Built to Scale

PlayFab is hosted on AWS, spans three “availability zones” for maximum uptime, and auto-scales. We’ve been tested past 1 million concurrent players, and 20,000 API calls-per-second.

Download stress test PDF »

We’re Here To Stay

We built this company for the long haul, but in the unlikely case we ever shut down our service, you get immediate access to our source code. It’s in our contract.

View clause 10.5 in our contract »
