Storyline

The Kidd Video band have their first adventure on the Flipside. As the band explores this strange cartoon world from the Kiddmobile, the mayor suddenly rushes toward them and causes them to crash. The young mayor has become old and speaks in unintelligibly rapid speech. The band later learns that he was trying to get their help. The evil music producer, Master Blaster, is angry that no one wants to go to the concerts of his aptly named band, the Copy Cats. He has gotten his revenge on the citizens of the Flipside by speeding up their lives, causing them to become prematurely old. Later, he plans to trap everyone at a Copy Cat concert by slowing them down to the point of immobility. The Copy Cats will be performing the music of Lionel Richie, whom Master Blaster has kidnapped and placed in a bubble along with Richie's fellow musicians. This bubble is connected to a machine that enables the Copy Cats to steal their talents and perform just like them. Written by J. Spurlin
