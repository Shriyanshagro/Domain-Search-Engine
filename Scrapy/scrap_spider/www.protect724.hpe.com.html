Announcement 1 Call for Papers and early-bird discount deadlines : Extended | Protect 2016
Announcement:Call for Papers and early-bird discount deadlines : Extended | Protect 2016
  • Show Details
  • Hide Details
by 00581365 (April 26, 2016)

The Protect 2016 call for papers has been *extended to May 6th 2016* and I encourage you to submit one for possible inclusion in our session offerings before the May 6th, 2016 deadline. Protect is our largest security event of the year, attracting over 2,000 attendees, and is an ideal venue for sharing your story with industry colleagues. It takes place September 13-16 at the Gaylord National in National Harbor, MD. 

 

The Protect 2016 early-bird discount price deadline has been *extended to June 15 2016*.  Follow the link to register take advantage of our early-bird discount.

 

Messaging tips

We seek presentation submissions that:

Support the theme of this year’s event: Protect your digital enterpriseEither focus on a technical tip or best-practice usage example of 

HPE Security software or share a successful business outcome of an HPE solution/service that attendees can leverage in their own organizations

Reveal how to use HPE Security software or other HPE solution to address the broadened, more diverse threat landscape and factoring in back-up and recovery

Help attendees transform their own businesses via digital security initiativesContain no sales or marketing pitches

 

Preference will be given to client-led sessions detailing their own security transformation vision, strategy and projects. We’ll select a balance of content that shares practical insights, advice and learnings from the perspective of the business as well as the technical implementation.  

 

How, when and why?

Please submit your paper before the May 6, 2016 deadline. Accepted primary speakers receive a free pass to Protect 2016, a $1,595 value.

You will be notified in May whether your session has been accepted for Protect 2016.  I hope you take advantage of this opportunity to showcase your security solution!

Failed login attempt after ArcMC 2.2GA Upgrade
Hello, after upgrading to ArcMC 2.2 GA you will see events like "Failed login attempt" from the admin user in the ArcMC audit logs. To solve this issue navigate to "Node Management" - "View all Nodes", click on "Default", under "Action" (on the right side) update the credentials for the admin user for each host. Cheers!
