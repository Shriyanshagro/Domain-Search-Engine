Applicability

Unless specifically noted otherwise, the following policies, standards, guidelines, and procedures apply to all departments, agencies, offices, boards, commissions, bureaus, authorities, and authorized individuals in the employment of the state responsible for the management, operation, or use of State IT resources, under the authority of the Code of Alabama 1975 (Title 41, Section 28, Articles 1-8).

These documents do not apply to counties, municipalities, the Alabama State Port Authority, or institutions of higher education governed by a separate board of trustees, unless these entities or institutions enter into cooperative agreements and/or contracts related to Information Technology efforts with the State.
