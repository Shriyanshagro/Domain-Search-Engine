    YPC Post Party

    Friday, May 13, 2016

    Join Young Patrons for an UPPER West Side Story. Wear your brightest shade of red while you enjoy ballet, hors d’oeuvres, and dancing. Party on the Promenade after the evening’s All Robbins’ program featuring Dances at a Gathering and West Side Story Suite.

    Host Committee: Laura Bacigalupo, Hayley Kucich, Nell Kucich, Cara Lonergan, Mitch Lowenthal, Rachel Mann, Daniel Spence, and Karl Yeh
    Artist Committee: Ashley Hod, Claire Kretzschmar, Lauren Lovette, Alexa Maxwell, and David Prottas

    Please indicate your seat location in the Special Requests field below.

    Are you a member? Your after party ticket is complimentary! To RSVP, email youngpatrons@nycballet.com.
