China, People's Republic of

Use the tabs below to find online resources about China from governmental and nongovernmental sources. Most of these sources are freely available to the public, but titles followed by a CU symbol are only accessible on campus or via VPN.

APEC Individual Action Plan - People's Republic of China
Report updated annually by each Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) Member Economy recording its actions that help realize the APEC goal.

Australian Government - China
Country profile, economic and trade information, and foreign relations from the Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade.

BBC News Profile - China
A guide to history, politics and economic background of countries from BBC News.
  • BBC News Profile - Tibet
CIA World Factbook - China
Country profile provides information on country background, geography, people, government, communications, transportation, military, and transnational issues.

FAO and China
Information on economic and agricultural issues from the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO). It also has a good selection of maps.

Human Development Report on China
Provides quick statistics on human development, economy, labor force, and population from the United Nations Development Programme.

IAEA - China
Country factsheet on multilateral and safeguard agreements from the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA).

IEA - China
Country information, energy-related statistics, graphs and publications from the International Energy Agency (IEA).

ITC - China
Country-specific trade and market profiles, map, and trade statistics from the International Trade Centre (ITC).

India Foreign Relations Brief - China
Country briefs from India's Ministry of External Affairs.

Library of Congress Country Study - China
This is a book-length report studying the history, social customs, and politics of the country from early times to the present.

OECD - China (Non-Member Economy)
Country information, statistics, publications, and news from the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD).

OHCHR - China
Country background, treaties, reporting status, calendar of events and publications related to human rights from the Office of the United Nations High Commissioner on Human Rights (OHCHR).

UNdata Country Profile - China
Provides summary, economic, social, and environmental statistics from the United Nations Statistics Division.

UNESCO Worldwide - China
Highlights cooperation between the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) and its Member States.
  • UNESCO Communication & Information - China
    Country profile, news and links to related projects and web sites.
  • UNESCO Institute for Statistics - China
  • A selection of national statistics on education, literacy, science and technology, culture, and communication.
  • UNESCO Office Beijing
    Cluster Office representing UNESCO in China, Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Japan, Mongolia and Republic of Korea in the East and South-East Asia Cluster.
  • UNESCO World Heritage - China
    Cultural and natural heritage properties and documents on the World Heritage List.
United Kingdom's Foreign and Commonwealth Office - China
Country profile covering history, politics, economy, human rights, relations with the UK, and travel.

United Nations Statistics Division: Environment Snapshot - China
Country snapshots offer national environment statistics.

U.S. Commercial Service - China
Country profile about doing business in China by the Commercial Service, the global business solutions unit of the Department of Commerce.
  • China Business Information Center
U.S. Department of Energy - China
Country analysis report from the Energy Information Administration.

U.S. Department of State - Background Note: Benin
This resource provides background information on issues such as the economy, history, politics, and foreign relations. The State Department also provides access to annual reports on human rights and terrorism.
The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
Provides information on the Communist Party of China, National People's Congress, Presidency, State Council, Central Military Commission, Supreme People's Court, and Supreme People's Procuratorate. Links to history, constitution, politics, economy, finance & banking, agriculture, environment, transportation & telecommunications, industry, tourism, education, science & technology, sports, culture, religion & ethnic affairs, and statistics.

Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) Declassified Documents
Declassified information available from the FOIA Electronic Reading Room. Enter "China" as the search term.
  • National Intelligence Council - China National Intelligence Estimates
    A collection of declassified products released by the Central Intelligence Agency (CIA).
  • National Intelligence Council (NIC) Declassified Documents
    Database of intelligence reports, National Intelligence Estimates, and other publications produced by the National Intelligence Council or its predecessor organizations, the Office of National Estimates and the Office of Reports and Estimates since 1946.
Crisis Group International Databases and Resources
Crisis Group provides analysis of violent conflicts and peace implementation.
  • CrisisWatch Database
    Monthly bulletin of significant situations of conflict or potential conflict around the world.
Congressional Research Service (CRS) Reports CU
The Congressional Research Service (CRS), the research arm of Congress, prepares brief reports on topics of interest to members of Congress. Enter "China" as the search term.

Declassified Documents Reference System CU
Search this database to find declassified documents related to China.

Defense Technical Information Center (DTIC)
Department of Defense Scientific and Technical Reports Collection provides access to citations to unclassified unlimited documents that have been entered into DTIC's Technical Reports (TR) Database, as well as the electronic full-text of many of these documents.

Digital National Security Archive CU
Database of primary declassified documents regarding critical U.S. policy decisions. Enter "China" as the search term.
  • Digital National Security Archive: China and the United States: From Hostility to Engagement, 1960-1998 CU This database has gathered material from the U.S. government and written essays on the relationship between China and the United States.
Facts on International Relations and Security Trends (FIRST)
An integrated database containing information on armed conflicts and peace keeping, arms production and trade, military expenditure, armed forces and weapons.

Global Legal Information Network (GLIN)
"Public database of official texts of laws, regulations, judicial decisions, and other complementary legal sources contributed by governmental agencies and international organizations."

Human Rights Watch - China and Tibet
News and reports related to human rights.

LexisNexis Academic Country & Region Reports CU
News, country briefings, and country risk guides. Filter by country or choose "Country & Region Reports" from publication type.

OECDiLibrary CU
The Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development's (OECD) online collection of statistical databases, books and periodicals.

Statistical Database System (SDBS) Online
SDBS is the the Asian Development Bank's central statistical database that stores macro-economic and social data of its developing member countries.

UNdata
UNdata provides a single entry point allowing users to search and download numerous statistical resources of the United Nations System.

U.S. Census Bureau Foreign Trade Statistics - China
U.S. trade in goods (imports, exports and balance) with China.

World dataBank
Contains data from the World Bank, including the World Development Indicators & Global Development Finance, Gender Statistics, Education Statistics, Health Nutrition and Population Statistics, African Development Indicators, Global Economic Monitor, and Millennium Development Goals. Users can select variables (some going as far back as 1960) to create customized tables, charts, maps, and reports.

Worldwide Incidents Tracking System (WITS)
WITS is the National Counterterrorism Center's database of terrorist incidents.
Asian Development Bank - People's Republic of China
Projects, publications, and news about China and the ADB.

Delegation of the European Commission to China
The Delegations of the External Service serve European Union interests throughout the world.

Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Washington, DC
Diplomatic representation of China in the United States.

Embassy of the United States in Beijing, China
Diplomatic representation of the U.S. in China.

EU's Relations with China
Overview, country strategy, EU support, and documents on the European Union's relations with China.

Group of Twenty (G-20)
The G-20 is a group of 19 of the world's largest economies and the European Union. The G-20 is an informal forum that promotes open and constructive discussion between industrial and emerging-market countries on key issues related to global economic stability.

G-77 at the United Nations
The Group of 77 is an intergovernmental organization of developing states in the UN. It provides the means for the countries of the South to articulate and promote their collective economic interests and enhance their joint negotiating capacity on all major international economic issues within the United Nations system, and promote South-South cooperation for development. There are now 130 member states (including China), although the name G-77 was retained because of its historical significance.

IMF - China
Country information, reports and publications from the International Monetary Fund.

Permanent Mission of the People's Republic of China to the United Nations
Web site of member state missions and their representatives to the United Nations.

ReliefWeb - China
ReliefWeb, administered by the UN Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs (OCHA), provides news, documents, maps, and background information on emergency assistance by country.

UNHCR
News, operations, statistics, background, analysis and policy, and maps from the Office of the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees. Select China from the "Browse by country" drop-down menu on the right.

United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) in China
UNDP helps countries work toward human rights, democratic governance, poverty reduction, crisis prevention and recovery, energy and environment for sustainable development, and HIV/AIDS prevention.

United Nations Division for Sustainable Development - China
Economic, social and environmental policies and plans for sustainable development from the UN Department Economic and Social Affairs.

United Nations in China
UN agencies and other institutions represented in China.

USAID - China
United States Agency for International Development (USAID) economic and humanitarian assistance programs.

U.S. Department of State - China
U.S.-Chinese relations, humanitarian assistance, reconstruction, and country background notes.
  • Background Notes - China
    Provides country background and foreign policy information for the American public from the Department of State's Bureau of Public Affairs.
World Bank - China
World Bank data, projects, and research in China.

World Trade Organization - China
Provides key information on trade statistics, WTO commitments, disputes, trade policy reviews, and notifications for member countries.
UNAIDS - China
Country situation analysis and news from the Joint United Nations Programme on HIV/AIDS.

WHO (World Health Organization)
  • WHO - China
    Information and statistics on the status of public health in China from the World Health Organization (WHO).
  • WHO-WPRO
    Official site of the World Health Organization Western Pacific Regional Office (WHO-WPRO). 
  • Country Health Information Profile - China
    Health situation analysis in China.
  • WHO Country Office in China
    China health information and program site.
Center for Defense Information (CDI) - China
Provides analysis, news and interviews on defense issues and China.

China Military Guide
Maps, agencies, military branches, nuclear capability, news, and references from GlobalSecurity.org.

International Crisis Group - China
Reports, briefings, and analysis of violent conflicts and peace implementation in the Taiwan Strait.

Military Policy Awareness Links (MiPALS) - China
MiPALs are published by the National Defense University (NDU) Library to provide access to key U.S. policy statements on the country or topic for Military Education Research Library Network (MERLN) users.

NTI - China Profile
Nuclear, biological, chemical and missile development programs, policies and activities from the Nuclear Threat Initiative (NTI). NTI is non-profit organization with a mission to strengthen global security and fulfill the Non-Proliferation Treaty's goals and ambitions. NTI's members are from the United States, Russia, Japan, India, Pakistan, China, Jordan, Sweden, France and the United Kingdom.

United Nations Security Council Permanent Member
The UN Security Council is responsible for the maintenance of international peace and security. The Council is composed of five permanent members — China, France, Russian Federation, the United Kingdom and the United States — and ten non-permament members that are elected by the General Assembly for two-year terms and not eligible for immediate re-election.

U.S. Department of Defense - Annual Report to Congress:
Military Power of the People's Republic of China
Annual report (2002-present) on current and future military strategy of the People's Republic of China.
