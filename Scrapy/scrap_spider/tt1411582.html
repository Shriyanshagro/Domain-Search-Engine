User Reviews

 
A short docu. about the people behind the leads for the moviebuff.
13 November 2015 | by shide_85 (Sweden) – See all my reviews

This is a documentary that only touches on the subject of (as they present it) character acting and actors. •If you are a moviebuff that were around during the 90's you will pretty much only see familiar faces. •If you're looking to find something to just spend a little time on it's still not a waste of it, as it touches on how the movieindustry used to work and works. •Finally, if you are an actor or would be actor you can pick a few tips and tricks (less than a handful, that are noteworthy) up for another day.

Pro's •Cast •Atmosphere(actors seem to be open to the questions posed) •Simplcity in the execution, suitable for what this is and skips annoyances

Con's •Short and very shallow •Editing - jumping around a bit too much between the clips
