Can you dual-boot Windows 7 with Windows 10?

You can, but if you upgraded from Windows 7 to Windows 10 Microsoft made your Windows 7 license expire 30 days later, and you will need to buy a copy of Windows 7. Even after you buy another copy of Windows 7, installing it from a flash drive or optical disc sometimes doesn't work.



Lincoln Spector addresses this in a recent PC World article that goes into more detail, if you are interested.
