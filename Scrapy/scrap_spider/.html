Powered by NYCC Constituent Services Data

Every year thousands of New Yorkers call their City Council Members seeking assistance. In 2016 New York City Council began publishing anonymized daily records of this casework. This is the data which powers CallNYC.org giving a live picture of work happening in New York City Council district offices.

Call NYC recognizes NYC Council Members who excel in service with the highest number of cases in each category from the last 365 days. To ensure quality data a case must have a borough, category, and subcategory to be counted.

Not all City Council members opt to publish their service data and different members use the system in different ways. As such CallNYC.org can only offer a picture of members who use the system. Explore the Constituent Services Data yourself on New York City Council's web site.
