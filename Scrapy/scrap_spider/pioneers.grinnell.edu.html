    Athletes of the Week
    Nate Kolacia
    Men's Track and Field
    Nate Kolacia from Fort Dodge, Iowa (St. Edmond), reeled off a time of 1:53.31 in the 800-meter run at last weekend’s Kip Janvrin Invitational.

    Kolacia’s time was just a hundredth-of-a-second off his school-record clocking and ranks 43rd nationally.
     
    Kayla Morrissey
    Softball
    Kayla Morrissey ’18, a 5-10 third baseman from Burr Ridge, Ill. (Lyons Township North), continued her red-hot season by hitting .462 in the last week, going 6-of-13 at the plate with two triples, five RBIs and three runs scored.

    She also had a perfect 100 percent fielding percentage with seven putouts and nine assists with no errors.
