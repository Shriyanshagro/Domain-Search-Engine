Lauren Fix

Writer | Actress
View Resume | Official Photos »
Lauren Fix was born on February 3, 1964 in Dearborn, Michigan, USA. She is a writer and actress, known for The Car Coach (2011), The Weather Channel Weekend View (2006) and Talk2DIY Automotive (1986). She has been married to Paul Fix II since October 14, 1989. They have two children. See full bio »
