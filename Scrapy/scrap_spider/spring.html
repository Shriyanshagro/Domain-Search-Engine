    Season & Tickets

    Wednesday May 4, 2016 7:00 PM

    Back to calendar

    Fresh from his Broadway directoral debut (An American in Paris), former NYCB Resident Choreographer Christopher Wheeldon returns to create a world premiere for the 2016 Spring Gala.

    Location:
    David H. Koch Theater
    Running Time:

    SPRING GALA

    • Concerto DSCH

      • Music by: Dmitri Shostakovich
      • Choreography by: Alexei Ratmansky
      • Principal Casting: MAY 4, 7 EVE, 18: Sara Mearns, Tyler Angle, Brittany Pollack (replaces Ana Sophia Scheller), Gonzalo Garcia, Anthony Huxley
        MAY 20, 21 MAT: Sterling Hyltin*, Adrian Danchig-Waring*, Brittany Pollack, Harrison Ball, Joseph Gordon* (*First time in role)


      With its thrilling Shostakovich score and dramatic texture, Concerto DSCH excels with classical ingenuity and contemporary stylishness.

    • Mothership (New Bates/Blanc)

      • Music by: Mason Bates
      • Choreography by: Nicolas Blanc
      • Principal Casting: MAY 4, 7 EVE, 18, 20, 21 MAT: Jackie Bologna*, Clara Frances*, Alston Macgill*+, Claire Von Enck*, Silas Farley*, Christopher Grant*+, Alec Knight*+, Sebastian Villarini-Velez* (*First time in role)(+NYCB apprentice)

      Prepare for liftoff with the four couples in Nicolas Blanc’s first work for NYCB as they launch into spinning lifts, swiveling walks, and racing slides danced to Mason Bates’ electro-acoustic orchestral score of the same name.

    • American Rhapsody (New Gershwin/Wheeldon)

      • Music by: George Gershwin
      • Choreography by: Christopher Wheeldon
      • Principal Casting: MAY 4, 7 EVE, 18, 20, 21 MAT: Tiler Peck*, Robert Fairchild*, Unity Phelan*, Amar Ramasar* (*First time in role)

      Tony Award-winner (An American in Paris) and former NYCB Resident Choreographer Christopher Wheeldon draws upon his recent Broadway success in his latest NYCB creation, set to Gershwin’s popular Rhapsody in Blue.
