Direction Group Dinner & Meeting

17-18 May 2016, Swift Group, Cottingham

This Direction Group Meeting will be hosted by the Swift Group at their manufacturing facilities in Cottingham, Hull. It will include a tour of their state of the art factory which produces, holiday homes, caravans and motorhomes. www.swiftgroup.co.uk

Dinner: 7pm, Mercure Hotel, Grange Park Lane,  Willerby, HU10 6EA
Factory Tour: 10am-12pm (lunch provided)
Meeting: 1pm – 3pm.

About Swift
Since the foundation of the Swift Group in 1964, the company’s policy has always been to produce innovative products that are well designed, constructed to the highest standards and provide outstanding value for money. In 2004, the Swift Group began to build on that success with the launch of its range of prestigious holiday homes in a venture forming the Company’s biggest investment since it started manufacturing motorhomes in 1985.

Over the last decade, as the Company has grown from a single brand, Swift, to a multi brand operation encompassing Sterling, Sprite, Bessacarr, Escape and Autocruise. A multi million pound investment programme in online technology enables dealers not only to order online, but also to check product availability and delivery dates

To register: please email roisin.sweeney@buildoffsite.com  registration for the dinner on the 17th will close on Tuesday 10th May

Suggested hotel: Mercure Hotel, Grange Park Lane,  Willerby, HU10 6EA  0844 8159037

 

 

25MAY

Homes & Offsite Supplier day

25 May 2016, BRE, Innovation Park, Watford

Join Barratts, Calfordseaden and St Gobain in collaboration with BuildOffsite and the Offsite Managment School on 25th May at the BRE Innovation Park Watford to hear how you can help them address this challenge and learn how Offsite processes can really make a difference.  The aim of the day is to provide you with an opportunity to:

  • Hear from Barrats about the challenges they face, their objectives, their future plans and understand how they need to work with organisations like yours to address these issues
  • Learn how market leaders such as St Gobain and B&K Structures are rising to the challenge and using innovative building solutions
  • Hear directly from architects such as Calford Seaden and Chapman Taylor Architects on why there is a need for more collaboration within the supply chain
  • Find out about free training on the use of offsite construction and how you can adopt these methodologies to meet your clients needs
  • Understand why 44 plus major contractors, clients and suppliers are recommending the Offsite Management School  as the ‘go to’ resource for skills development

Places are limited and will be given on a first come first serve basis so please click here to secure your free place

If you have any questions just email amanda.armstrong@supplychainschool.co.uk or call the team on 0191 389 8496
