Join us for the 27th ICDE World Conference

Toronto, Canada October 17-19, 2017

Click here to sign up for the ICDE 2017 newsletter to receive the latest info on the conference.

The 27th edition of the International Council for Open and Distance Education (ICDE) World Conference will be held in Toronto, Canada in October 2017.

Join faculty members/instructors from colleges and universities, practitioners, experts, policy-makers, academic decision-makers and technology providers involved in the field of online and distance education from around the world for this bi-annual summit dedicated to online learning and distance education.

Toronto, which is believed to mean “meeting place”, is the ideal host city for ICDE 2017 World Conference. It is the largest city in Canada, boasting a population of 2.5 million, and is heralded as one of the most multicultural cities in the world with over 140 languages and dialects spoken.

The city boasts vibrant ethnic neighbourhoods providing a rich cultural experience for visitors, not to mention great food and entertainment.

Mandla S. Makhanya ICDE President
Gard Titlestad Secretary General of ICDE
Maxim Jean-Louis President - Chief Executive Officer Contact North | Contact Nord

From Africa, Arab States, Asia and the Pacific, Europe and North America, Latin America and the Caribbean, we are all going to Toronto, Canada to join Contact North | Contact Nord, host of the 27th ICDE World Conference, October 17-19, 2017.
