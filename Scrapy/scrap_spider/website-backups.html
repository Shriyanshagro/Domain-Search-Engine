Website Backups

Easy off-site storage of files.

Regardless of the controls you put in place, you can never be 100% certain that all the implemented safeguards will work. As a final precaution, you should always account for the value and importance of having a backup of your website and associated files.



Depending on your platform, you have a wide array of options available to you when it comes to backing up your website. The Value of Backups

The value of having a good backup cannot be over-emphasized. They are the website owner's ultimate insurance policy when all things go wrong. When the malware destroys the website or the attacker decides it's best to delete the entire site, backups will often be the best solution to leverage. Even if the backups are infected, being able to have a stored copy of the code will prove invaluable to any website owner.

Depending on the platform your website is built on, there are undoubtedly a number of viable solutions available. It's best to engage your respective community for insights into the best solution for your specific case.

Additionally, most hosting providers offer a free backup service for 24 hours; the feature often is not enabled by default so talk to your hosting provider about their backup procedures.
