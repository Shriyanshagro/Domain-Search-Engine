Industry Services

We give you decades of IT experience meeting customer demands in a wide range of industries worldwide.

The Help You Need to Operate Nimbly and Innovate Faster than the Competition

Many of the world’s largest companies turn to Hewlett Packard Enterprise for innovative, growth-enabling, cost-reducing business solutions.

We provide holistic IT expertise across platforms, vendors and technologies—from software-defined Compute, to unified networking and cloud integration, to application transformation and virtualized data management.

Communication service providers (CSPs) need to scale against explosive network growth. Banks must deploy secure and personalized mobile apps. Utilities need to continuously develop more customer-centric operations. Healthcare providers want to create retail-like experiences. Hewlett Packard Enterprise is the partner they turn to because we create successful, highly customized solutions that transform our customers into efficient digital marketers, customer experience heroes and safe, stable service providers.

Advise

With thousands of industry experts, our IT consulting services team can develop solutions for every aspect of your business, across vendors and platforms.

Transform

Complexities and challenges become new opportunities with the right technology. Disrupt your industry in the always connected, all-digital world.

Manage

Keep your reputation high and costs down with a range of IT Outsourcing (ITO) services and Business Process Services.

Communications, Media and Entertainment

Communications, Media and Entertainment

Communication Service Providers (CSPs) are under increased market pressure to search for new revenue streams as their value proposition becomes commoditized. They need to grow their business faster than the explosion of network traffic and competition from nimble over-the-top (OTT) businesses.

Choose from a range of solutions to bridge the CSP technology gap and compete in the new business of the network:
  • Reduce capital and operational expenses with Network Functions Virtualization (NFV)
  • Exploit the Internet of Things (IoT) with new applications
  • Monetize content and deliver better customer experience
