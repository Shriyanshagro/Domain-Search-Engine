The Forrester Wave™: Private Cloud Software Suites, Q1 2016 provides a comparative evaluation of private cloud solution vendors and an overview of current trends in the private cloud space. In this report, Forrester names Hewlett Packard Enterprise a “Leader” and one of two vendors with “solid current offerings delivering great strength in functionality across almost every category.” It is our opinion that this distinction reflects our foresight in recognizing that the road to hybrid starts with private cloud, as well as our ongoing commitment to providing high-performing private cloud solutions as part of your hybrid mix.
