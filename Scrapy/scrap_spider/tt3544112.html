User Reviews

 
Fabulous
13 April 2016 | by Cole Waters (Cole.waters@icloud.com) (United States) – See all my reviews

This is the first film so far of the year that will be an academy award nominee. It was corky and just full of heart and great music. Sing Street is pushing the PG-13 rating to a R rated film, but it was still a really good film. The acting was absolutely flaw less from start to finish.

It had a lot of good music in it that is still in my head this minute. I bet Sing Street will get nominated at least for 8 Oscars and 9 Golden Globes.

Now parents there is a lot of rude and sexual content in this movie that could be effective on the little ones. The main audience I think is older teens and up from what I saw at my showing of this masterpiece. This will be a movie that will be a film of the century and empire forever it was great

GO SEE IT
