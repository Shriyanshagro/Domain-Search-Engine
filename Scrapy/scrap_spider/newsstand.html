                          Description

                          Start with a 14 day free trial – subscribe and download your first issue today. You are free to choose to cancel during your trial period or do nothing to start your paid subscription and never miss an issue.

                          Money magazine helps you manage your finances by cutting through the jargon to deliver clear and precise information to help you save money and make the most of your investments. Each issue, you'll enjoy credible, well-researched reports and expert commentary from some of Australia's most respected financial writers.

                          + Exact digital replica
                          + Read using the Google Play Magazines app on your Android phone and tablet
                          + Switch between magazine layout and an easy-to-read text only view for smaller screens
                          + Read on your computer using Google Chrome

                          ***”Get 3 months for just $3” promotional offer on monthly subscriptions is for the first 3 months only. Subscribers will be charged the standard issue cost after the promotion ends, from the 4th month onwards.
