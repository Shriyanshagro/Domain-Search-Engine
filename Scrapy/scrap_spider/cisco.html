Cisco ASA Specialist Boot Camp

Intense School is excited to offer the Certified Cisco ASA Specialist (Cisco ASA ®) Boot Camp, an extensive and intense 9-day course designed to prepare professionals for further advancement of their skills and talent. Intense School's Cisco ASA Boot Camp focuses on preparing students on the design and implementation of Cisco secure networks, while teaching basic and advanced configuration and integration of the security devices for your network.

Cisco is one of the fastest innovators in the security space, and this class, complete with training on the cutting edge “Adaptive Security Appliance” family of devices, keeps pace with the times, and assures that you will do the same!

This course is taught by the most qualified Cisco ASA  instructors. All instructors used for this course are CCSI’s (Certified Cisco System Instructor). And you know that when you go with Intense School's, the instructors are also meeting customers’ needs in the field, deploying Cisco security in heterogeneous environments.

Register today and take the next step in furthering your Cisco® certification and status to a higher level of professionalism!

Course Description:
Only Intense School's Authorized Cisco ASA Specialist Boot Camp offers you the following benefits:

  • Skills to defend networks against attacks
  • Greater understanding of how to meet an organization’s security needs
  • Proper education and tools to prepare for Cisco  ASA  certification
  • Three (3) Certification exams (IINS, SNAF and SNAA)
  • Hands-on exposure to the latest and greatest technology, including the ASA 5000 series security appliances
  • Achieve THREE Premium Cisco Certifications:
  • IINS (Implementing Cisco IOS Network Security)
  • SNAF (Securing Networks with ASA Foundation)
  • SNAA (Securing Networks with ASA Advanced)

Course Objectives:

  • Thoroughly understand network security threats, procedures and transactions
  • Successfully manage firewalls, virtual private networks (VPNs), VPN hardware and software clients, and other critical security systems
  • Successfully manage Cisco’s most recent invention, the Adaptive Security Appliance (ASA)
  • Learn how to design and build a secure network
  • Plan and execute levels of security departments
  • Configure Cisco firewalls
  • Configure Cisco Anti-Virus on an ASA
  • Configure Cisco VPNs, server and client
  • Extensive knowledge of configuring Internet Protocol Security (IPSec) technologies and networks

 
 
Prerequisites

Prior to enrolling in our Authorized Cisco ASA Specialist boot camp, students should have a sound working experience with basic network security, TCP/IP, and encryption technologies. To achieve Cisco ASA Specialist status, student must possess a valid CCNA certification. It is recommended that students attending this intensive boot camp experience already possess some exposure to administering Cisco Security devices.

 
Who Should Attend:

  • Network Administrators
  • Firewall Administrators
  • Security Professionals
