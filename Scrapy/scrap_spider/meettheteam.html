Meet The Team

Joe Mahoney / Rocky Mountain PBS

The Inside Energy reporting team, from left Leigh Paterson, Stephanie Joyce, Alisa Barba, Dan Boyce, Jordan Wirfs-Brock and Emily Guerin.

Alisa Barba

Alisa Barba, Executive Editor
abarba@insideenergy.org
@alisabarba

Alisa Joyce Barba is an award-winning journalist, producer, writer and editor with 25 years experience in both network and public broadcasting. Prior to IE, she served as Senior Editor for Fronteras: the Changing America Desk, a regional news collaboration project involving seven public radio stations across the southwest.  She also served as Western Bureau Chief for NPR for 12 years.

Dan Boyce

Dan Boyce, Colorado Reporter
dboyce@insideenergy.org
@boycedan

Dan Boyce comes to the Inside Energy team after five years of television and radio reporting in his home state of Montana. In his most recent role as Montana Public Radio’s Capitol Bureau Chief, Dan produced daily stories on state politics and government.

Laura Frank

Laura Frank, Executive Supervisor
lfrank@rmpbs.org
@laura_frank

Laura Frank is Vice President of News at Rocky Mountain PBS. I-News delivers multimedia reports to news outlets across the Rocky Mountain region and is a founding member of the national nonprofit Investigative News Network.

Emily Guerin

Emily Guerin, North Dakota Reporter
eguerin@insideenergy.org
@guerinemily

Emily Guerin comes to Inside Energy after working as a correspondent at High Country News, a magazine that reports on energy and environmental issues in the American West. She was also the interim news director for KVNF, a community radio station serving rural Western Colorado.

Rebecca Jacobson

Rebecca Jacobson, Producer
rjacobson@insideenergy.org
@rebeccajacobson

Rebecca Jacobson comes to Inside Energy after seven years as a science reporter, videographer, editor and producer for PBS NewsHour. She started working in public television at NET in her home state of Nebraska. Since then she has covered stories on politics, health care, the arts, the environment, science and engineering for the web, radio and television.

Stephanie Joyce

Stephanie Joyce, Energy and Natural Resources Reporter
sjoyce@insideenergy.org
@stephaniemjoyce

Stephanie Joyce reports on energy and natural resources for Wyoming Public Radio. Before joining WPR, she was the news director at KUCB, a public radio station in Alaska's Aleutian Islands. She's a former Metcalf Institute Science Reporting fellow and the recipient of a Middlebury Environmental Journalism fellowship. Stephanie contributes many of her reports to Inside Energy.

Leigh Paterson

Leigh Paterson, Reporter
lpaterson@insideenergy.org
@Sclaterpee

Leigh Paterson was raised in New Jersey, graduated from Lehigh University in Pennsylvania, and then taught English at a culinary high school in France. Leigh then got her Master's in Broadcast Journalism from the S.I Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University, and then moved to Washington D.C. in 2009. After spending two years as a producer at CanadianTV's Washington bureau, Leigh left to freelance. Since then, as a one woman show, she has reported for TV and radio from across the country for BBC News, BBC World Service, PRI's the World, ABC-Univision, Agence France Presse, and CBC News.

Amber Rivera

Amber Rivera, Engagement Editor
arivera@insideenergy.org
@InsideEnergyNow, @amber_waves

Amber Rivera connects our reporting with local communities, and manages reader experience and engagement with our content across all digital platforms. She is a longtime lover and student of energy and environmental issues. Prior to joining IE she worked for a start-up solar financing company, and also advised utility companies, governments, and the World Bank on the financing of electricity infrastructure. She holds a B.S. in Science, Technology, and International Affairs from Georgetown University.

Jordan Wirfs-Brock

Jordan Wirfs-Brock, Data Journalist
jordanwb@insideenergy.org
@jordanwb

Jordan Wirfs-Brock is a data journalist and civic technologist whose passion is telling stories with data and empowering others to do the same. She previously worked in the Piton Foundation's Data Initiative, where she used neighborhood-level data to help people improve their communities.

Listen To U.S. Coal Production Fall Off A Cliff

America’s coal industry is hurting: In the past year, thousands of workers have been laid off and a majority of the country’s major coal companies have filed for bankruptcy. Coal production is at 30-year low.

More Coal Layoffs In Wyoming

Bankrupt coal company Alpha Natural Resources confirmed that it laid off 37 employees today from its two Powder River Basin coal mines. These cuts follow much larger layoffs last month at two of the country's largest mines, owned by Arch Coal and Peabody Energy.

In Case You Missed It

Inside Energy Is A National Hub For Energy Reporting

Seven public radio and television stations from the nation’s energy epicenter in Colorado, Wyoming and North Dakota created this collaborative reporting center called Inside Energy. IE provides reporting that moves beyond polarized arguments and emotional debate to explore the points of tension, the tradeoffs and opportunities, and the very human consequences of energy policy, production, use and innovation.
