  2. Paulding County Farm Bureau 4-H Camp Scholarship Offered

    Check out the opportunity from Paulding County Farm Bureau to assist Paulding County 4-Hers wanting to attend 4-H Camp in July. Parents of the 4-Hers applying for this scholarship will need to have been Paulding County Farm Bureau members for at least 120 days to apply.

  3. Paulding County Agriculture, Master Gardener & 4-H Scholarships.

    The Paulding County Agricultural Scholarship is a yearly scholarship (approximately $200) is funded with interest from a $5000 gift in November of 1995 from the Paulding County Agronomy Committee, Inc. to the Paulding County 4-H and Youth Endowment Fund.
