ISO Connect is for people working within the ISO system who want to know more about, or participate in, ISO's general activities. It provides news and high-level information about ISO initiatives, excluding the technical work. ISO Connect can help you find information you need and connect you with people who can tell you more.

None of the above, but I still want to access ISO Connect
ISO Connect is reserved for people working within the ISO System. Please send an email to the ISO international helpdesk at helpdesk@iso.org explaining why you would like access.
