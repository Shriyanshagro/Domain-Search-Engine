Wipro in Canada

Wipro’s in-depth knowledge of the Canadian business environment, combined with our global experience, industry expertise and demonstrated capabilities, enable us to create and implement tailored, best-practice-based solutions that help our Canadian clients achieve operational excellence.

Client Value Enablement

Our Client Value Enablement Strategy links client-value with more effective and efficient delivery capabilities (onshore, nearshore and offshore) to deliver the optimal balance between expertise, collaboration, risk tolerance, and price/cost savings to provide the best business results.

The ability to leverage Canadian talent while providing the right supporting resources to projects and accessing integrated global assets located throughout the world enables agility, innovation and world-class solution delivery for Canadian businesses.

This advantage means that Canada-focused operations get global, best-of-breed solutions that yield better waste reduction, improved operational efficiencies and increased margins.

Diversity & Inclusion

Wipro is committed to treating each individual equally, with dignity and respect. We strongly believe in and drive socio-cultural integration, and providing equal opportunity. We are committed to meeting the needs of persons with disabilities in a timely and sensitive manner, and will continue to do so by preventing and removing barriers to accessibility.

Our standard meets the accessibility requirements under the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act (“AODA”) and Wipro embraces the principles of the Act across all our Canadian office locations.

The requirements of the AODA are consistent with Wipro’s Global Diversity and Inclusion Framework to create and maintain a barrier-free community for all who come to Wipro to work, visit our locations, or access our goods and services. In Canada, all our Wipro employees are required to complete an accessibility learning program. This helps us to ensure that our employees’ interactions and practices meet the provincial accessibility standards.
