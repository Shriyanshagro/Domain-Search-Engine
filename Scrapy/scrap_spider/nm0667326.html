Rob Paulsen

Actor | Soundtrack | Music Department
Rob Paulsen was born on March 11, 1956 in Detroit, Michigan, USA as Robert Fredrick Paulsen III. He is an actor, known for Animaniacs (1993), Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles (2012) and The Mask (1995). He is married to Parrish Todd. They have one child. He was previously married to Carol Anne Schnarr. See full bio »
