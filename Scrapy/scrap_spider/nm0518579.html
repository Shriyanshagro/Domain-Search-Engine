Ulli Lommel

Actor | Writer | Director
View Resume | Official Photos »
Widely known for his frequent collaborations with Rainer Werner Fassbinder, a creative partnership which lasted 10 years and produced over 20 films, Ulli Lommel is one of the most consistently creative filmmakers to come from the New German Cinema movement. The son of German comic performer Ludwig Manfred Lommel, Ulli Lommel began his career in ... See full bio »
