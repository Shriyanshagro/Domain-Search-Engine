U.S. Gaming Industry Sets High Standard for AML Compliance

Ahead of a major international evaluation of the United States’ system for preventing criminal abuse of the financial system, the American Gaming Association (AGA) released the most comprehensive examination ever conducted of the U.S. casino gaming industry’s compliance with anti-money laundering (AML) compliance.

Sports Betting in America

Following months of study and deliberation, the AGA’s Board of Directors issued a set of recommendations in 2015 that marked a major shift in the industry’s approach to sports betting, which is currently illegal in all but four states. AGA is building a broad coalition that will determine whether a rational alternative to current sports betting law exists.
