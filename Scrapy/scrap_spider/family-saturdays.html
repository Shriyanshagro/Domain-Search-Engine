    Season & Tickets

    Saturday May 14, 2016 11:00 AM

    Back to calendar

    Most people picture tutus, tiaras, and pointe shoes when they think of ballet, but ballet is so much more than just that! Join us as we learn what ballet is today. NYCB dancers and orchestra members will perform ballets by contemporary choreographers and more in this one hour Saturday morning presentation designed especially for families. Your child may just learn some new choreography too!

    Family Saturdays: NYCB Now is generously supported by MetLife Foundation.

    Please note: Tickets are required for infants, children, and adults.

    Location:
    David H. Koch Theater
    Running Time:
    1 Hr.
