Laila Ali

Actress
Laila Ali was born on December 30, 1977 in Miami Beach, Florida, USA. She is an actress, known for American Gladiators (2008), Daddy's Girl (2007) and All in with Laila Ali (2013). She has been married to Curtis Conway since July 22, 2007. They have two children. She was previously married to John McClain. See full bio »
