Join the open cloud ecosystem

Deliver hybrid cloud with an unparalleled portfolio of cloud services, geographic reach, and quality of service

Join the network

Hybrid cloud adoption is continuing to grow at a rapid rate as enterprises seek the benefits of combining public and private cloud capabilities to differentiate themselves among increasing competition. A majority of enterprises are coming to recognize that they simply can’t achieve the full measure of their business goals by consuming only one kind of cloud.

But there are obstacles for service providers and ISVs as they strive to meet these enterprise cloud service needs, including narrow geographic reach due to varying regulations, the lack of a technology platform that supports innovation, and limited experience with cloud-native applications. HPE Helion Network helps remove these obstacles, so service providers and their enterprise customers can achieve hybrid cloud success.

 

Meet the HPE Helion Network

The HPE Helion Network is a global ecosystem of service providers, ISVs, and VARs dedicated to delivering open standards-based hybrid cloud services to enterprise customers. Built on a shared set of beliefs that embrace open source, multi-vendor solutions and collaborative innovation, the HPE Helion Network helps you actively participate in increasing hybrid cloud adoption. Through our ecosystem, you gain access to an expanded set of cloud services and improve your ability to meet country-specific data regulations. And you have the resources to offer open, secure, and agile hybrid IT solutions that prevent vendor lock-in and enable workload portability between on- and off-premises environments.

Benefit from a global network

Establishing a hybrid environment solves many of the cloud challenges businesses face, but complying with differing regulations across regions and localities often limits delivery capabilities. Built in response to worldwide data sovereignty concerns, HPE Helion Network is an ecosystem of interested parties working together to solve these overarching cloud challenges.

Membership in the HPE Helion Network presents opportunities for reduced costs and new revenue streams by allowing you to expand your cloud services portfolio with offerings from within the network, enhance your go-to-market with HPE’s direct sales and indirect channel partners, and reduce CAPEX and development costs by accessing cloud services sourced in geographies where you don’t have a physical presence.

As part of HPE’s commitment to the success of the network, HPE provides significant resources, including dedicated consulting, business development assistance, and sales support to help build, deliver, and operate enterprise-grade cloud services.

Read the solution brief

Service Providers

Service providers in the HPE Helion Network offer a wide range of compelling, flexible, and secure solutions that are based on open standards.

ISVs

Independent software vendors (ISVs) in the HP Helion Network deliver cloud services and apps enabled "as-a-service" that target essential workloads for key segments.

VARs

Value-added resellers (VARs) in the HP Helion Network provide leading solutions for their customers by pulling from our expansive menu of services and solutions for the sales, provisioning, maintenance, and invoicing of cloud services.
