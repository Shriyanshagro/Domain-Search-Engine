How do RSS feeds work?

RSS stands for Really Simple Syndication. An RSS feed is a news service that you can subscribe to, and which will automatically let you know whenever an internet service has been updated. Similar to a news ticker, the headlines of new articles appear on a user’s PC or other device, together with a short excerpt of the article text and a link to the original Web page. By following the link, you can see the information in full.

RSS steht für Really Simple Syndication, was mit "sehr einfache Informationsübermittlung" übersetzt werden kann. Feed hingegen lässt sich mit füttern oder versorgen übersetzen.

Giesecke & Devrient (G&D) develops, produces, and distributes products and solutions in the payment, secure communication, and identity management sectors. The Group's customer base mainly comprises central and commercial banks, mobile network operators, business enterprises, governments, and public authorities.
