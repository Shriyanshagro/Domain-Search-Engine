Old Mission
Santa Barbara

We were extremely excited when Old Mission Santa Barbara entrusted us with creating their new website and brand identity. Admiringly called the “Queen of Missions,” we wanted to capture her beauty and charm by giving the website a fresh and recognizable look.

In addition to designing the new logo and building a brand new website, we developed an e-commerce platform for their gift shop and made the site mobile phone-friendly. As a Santa Barbara web design company, we couldn’t be more excited to call Old Mission Santa Barbara a client of ours. It has been a pleasure working with these people, and we admire everything they do in and for the Santa Barbara community.
