bioplastics MAGAZINE is organizing the 4th PLA World Congress

PLA World Congress

PLA is one of the bioplastics with the largest market significance. The versatile bioplastics raw material is made almost completely from renewable resources. It is being used for packaging applications, for fibres in woven and non-woven applications. Even the automotive industry and consumer electronics are already applying PLA. Blending PLA with other bioplastics or other blend-partners as well as mixing it with natural fibres such as flax, hemp or kenaf broadens the range of applications even more.

That‘s why bioplastics MAGAZINE is now organizing the 4th PLA World Congress.

Experts from all involved fields will share their knowledge and contribute to a comprehensive overview of today‘s opportunities and challenges and discuss the possibilities, limitations and future prospects of PLA for all kind of applications.

REGISTER NOW

The 2 full-day-conference will be held on the 24th and 25th of May 2016 in Munich, Germany

 

"Bavarian Night" - On Tuesday May 24, 2016

bioplastics MAGAZINE invites all PLA World Congress delegates to a "Bavarian Night" in the famous HOFBRÄUHAUS.

Conferences by bioplastics MAGAZINE


This is the 4th PLA World Congress by bioplastics MAGAZINE.

Before we have successfully organized the the 1st and 2nd PLA Bottle Conference (2007 and 2008), 1st, 2nd and 3rd PLA World Congress as well as the Bioplastics Business Breakfast (during Düsseldorf K-Show in 2010 and 2013). In 2015 we held our first bio!PAC in Amsterdam and bio!CAR in Stuttgart
