Already a member of WCET? Get Involved with YOUR Cooperative

Participate in our popular email lists:

WCETNews is a moderated list used to distribute WCET's popular Article Digest, job posting from WCET members, announcements and breaking news of general interest to our members.

WCETDiscuss is a moderated discussion list reserved for WCET members. The list puts you in touch with higher education professionals from institutions and organizations in the U.S. and Canada. Have a question about how others address a policy issue or design solutions to certain educational technology challenges? This list is a quick and easy way to get ideas and input from your peers.

Engage in these discussions by subscribing today.

Volunteer to Serve

In addition to the opportunity to run for a position on one of our leadership committees, WCET also has several ad hoc and special purpose committees. WCET offers a number of ways for members to get more deeply involved in shaping WCET projects, which can be a rewarding experience both professionally and personally for those who volunteer. Contact WCET to tell us of your interests and to learn about opportunities to participate with WCET.

WCET Webcasts: Members gain free and priority access to webcasts. Join us on  the third Thursday of January, February, March, April, August,and September to learn about timely topics, promising practices, and engage in interesting conversations. Additional dates may be added.
