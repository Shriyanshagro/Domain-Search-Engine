International Powerlifting World Championships

Share with a friend
Print
Description

Aurora is hosting the premier powerlifting event of the year at the Radisson Hotel Denver Southeast.  Aurora will welcome international athletes to town to compete in the benchpress, dead lift, and squat.  Come see how many world recorders get shattered over the six-day event.

Event Date

Monday November 03 - Saturday November 08, 2014

Event Time

All day
