DATA

SERVICE SUPPORT

1-844-DISA-HLP (347-2457), Option 2
disa.dccc@mail.mil
disa.scott.conus.mbx.dccc@mail.smil.mil

The Data Services portfolio provides best effort IP-based services across the DoD enterprise based on the classification level of the information accessible, including Sensitive but Unclassified (SBU), Secret and Top Secret/Sensitive Compartmented Information (TS/SCI).

  • Sensitive but Unclassified (SBU) IP Data
    (formerly known as NIPRNet)
  • Secret IP Data
    (formerly known as SIPRNet)
  • Top Secret/Sensitive Compartmented Information Data (TS/SCI IP Data)
    (formerly known as JWICS)
